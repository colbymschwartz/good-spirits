# Good Spirits — Feature Ideas & Roadmap

## 🎤 Voice & AI
- **Voice stock My Bar** — tap mic, say what you have, fuzzy-match to ingredients, auto-toggle
- **Photo scan bar shelf** — take photo of your bar, AI identifies bottles, auto-stocks My Bar
- **AI cocktail suggestions** — "I'm in the mood for something smoky and refreshing" → AI recommends

## 👥 Social / Backend (requires Firebase/Supabase)
- **Global ratings** — aggregated user ratings visible to everyone
- **Community photos** — see other users' photos of drinks they've made
- **User-submitted recipes** — submit custom cocktails for review, publish to everyone
- **User accounts** — Apple Sign-In, sync across devices
- **Comments/discussion** per cocktail
- **Leaderboards** — most cocktails made, highest rated
- **Push notifications** — new cocktails added, trending drinks

## 🎨 Design & UX
- **Photo drag-to-position** — adjust crop/position after taking a photo
- **Placeholder photo** — subtle cocktail glass outline before photo is added
- **Half-star ratings** — more granular than whole stars
- **"7 vars" → "7 variations"** — more descriptive label
- **Popular/Classic sort** — sort by popularity or "classic" status, not just alphabetical
- **Seasonal recommendations** — tiki in summer, hot toddies in winter (beyond DOTD)
- **Color-coded spirit icons** — different background tints per spirit type

## 📱 App Features
- **Delete custom cocktails** — swipe-to-delete or long-press to remove custom cocktails from My Cocktails
- **Shopping list** — based on "Almost There" drinks, generate a shopping list of missing ingredients
- **Cocktail of the Week email** — opt-in weekly email with a featured cocktail
- **Share card improvements** — add the user's photo to the share card if they have one
- **Multiple bar profiles** — "Home Bar" vs "Travel Kit" vs "Party Setup"
- **Pairing suggestions** — food pairings for cocktails
- **Timer** — built-in timer for stirring/shaking (30 sec, 15 sec, etc.)
- **Unit converter** — oz ↔ ml ↔ cl toggle
- **Cocktail quiz/game** — "Name that cocktail" from ingredients
- **Ingredient substitutions** — "Don't have Campari? Try Aperol"

## 🏪 Monetization Ideas
- **Free tier** — 30 cocktails, basic features
- **Premium unlock** — full database, voice stock, AI suggestions, multiple bars
- **One-time purchase** vs subscription — TBD
- **Bar partnership** — featured cocktails from partner bars

## 🔧 Technical
- **Offline mode** — already works offline, but document it
- **iPad support** — optimize layout for iPad
- **Android release** — Expo makes this easy, just need to set up Google Play
- **Widget** — iOS home screen widget showing Drink of the Day
- **Apple Watch** — quick recipe glance on wrist
- **Siri Shortcuts** — "Hey Siri, what's the drink of the day?"
