# Privacy Policy — Good Spirits

**Last Updated:** March 22, 2026

## Overview
Good Spirits is a cocktail recipe app. Your privacy matters to us.

## Data Collection
Good Spirits does **not** collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All app data (favorites, ratings, My Bar ingredients, custom cocktails, photos, and notes) is stored **locally on your device only**.

## No Accounts Required
Good Spirits does not require user accounts, login, or registration.

## No Analytics
Good Spirits does not use analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs.

## Photos
Photos taken within the app are stored locally on your device. They are never uploaded to any server.

## Third-Party Services
Good Spirits does not integrate with any third-party services that collect user data.

## Changes
We may update this policy as the app evolves. Updates will be posted here.
